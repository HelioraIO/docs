title: "Earn Widget"
description: "The Earn Widget gives LPs a way to quickly deploy into Heliora opportunities."
---

The widget is a compact Heliora App that allows LPs to quickly find opportunities and deploy liquidity.

See the example widget [here](#).

The Earn Widget provides the fastest method for Heliora Distribution Partners to share Heliora opportunities to their audience. Simply login to the **Client Portal** and once approved, generate your custom link in your Distribution Dashboard.

As of 05-30 the initial widget v1 is live, but more updates will make the experience more robust including custom styling, specific deal/vault selection, and portfolio management.
